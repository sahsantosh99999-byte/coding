About Me

I am Santosh Kumar Sah, a Civil Engineer and Compex-Scholar pursuing my B.Tech at Jain Deemed-to-be University, Bengaluru. Born in Gaur, Nepal, my journey has taken me across borders — from achieving top grades in Nepal to hands-on engineering projects across India.

My professional experience spans project management at Toyota Kirloskar Motor, site engineering at Kailash Construction, and on-ground oversight at Unique Engineering Consultancy in Nepal. Each role has sharpened my ability to deliver results on site and under pressure.

Beyond practice, I am a published researcher with papers on carbon nanotube applications in construction and real-time landslide detection systems — and a patent holder for an on-demand land information mobile application.
